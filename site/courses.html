Study areas

Our programs

Adult classes

Our language programs for adults combine a diversity of teaching techniques that focus on a communicative approach and provide students with the necessary skills and concepts to feel comfortable to speak and understand others in a foreign language environment.

Children classes

Our language center currently provides children group classes to learn a variety of different world languages. Children are naturally more "efficient" foreign language learners and have a great capacity to learn a second language.

School programs

Our school programs are divided into themes. The program duration is 10 weeks. During each program we introduce two themes and a series of games and activities that develop the skills needed to learn a second language in a consistent and sequential manner.

Private lessons

A private lesson is a great option for those students who are learning a language with a specific goal in mind or those who prefer the one-on-one instruction without distractions. Lessons can be arranged for one student or more.

Private lessons allow the student or students to focus on any aspect that they would like to study - for example, conversational skills, grammar, cultural exposure, certain vocabulary, or the like; or simply learn a language in a private setting.
